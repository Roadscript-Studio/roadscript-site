About Roadscript

Engineering systems for authenticity and trust.

Roadscript is an independent product and engineering initiative focused on invisible watermarking for image authenticity. The project combines local-first engine design, verification-oriented workflows, and practical product surfaces for creators, operators, and downstream tools. The current website and demo communicate the direction of the system while backend execution, upload handling, and packaged releases continue to mature.
