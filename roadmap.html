Roadmap stages

A staged path from public preview to product workflows.

The public website is one milestone in a broader product direction. The roadmap stays focused on making the next steps legible without overstating what is already packaged.

Current stage

Website and sample verification demo

Establish a clear product surface, a recruiter-safe public site, and a consistent verification workflow using sample-driven results and a typed backend contract.

Next stage

Backend engine integration

Replace mock responses with real embed and verify execution, beginning with controlled sample inputs and a stable result contract.

About Roadscript

Engineering authenticity infrastructure with a user-facing verification layer.

Roadscript is an independent media authenticity initiative focused on embedding invisible signals directly into content. Cyphra is the user-facing verification product layer designed to help people interpret whether Roadscript-authenticated media still carries a valid signal. The current website and demo are public previews intended to communicate the roadmap, product direction, and engineering priorities while packaged releases and deeper integrations continue to mature.
