About Roadscript

Engineering systems for authenticity and trust.

Roadscript is an independent engineering initiative focused on building robust and verifiable systems for digital media. At its core is Cyphra, an invisible watermarking engine based on transform-domain techniques (DWT, DCT, and QIM) designed to ensure image authenticity while preserving visual quality. Alongside this, Roadscript explores confusion-aware learning through Confusion Memory, a research direction that models how systems learn from ambiguity, as well as low-level system design through MiniCore and MiniOS. From capture to verification and interpretation, Roadscript reflects a single goal: to design systems that are reliable, transparent, and grounded in real-world use.
